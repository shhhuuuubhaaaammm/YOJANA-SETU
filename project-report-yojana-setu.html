Project Description

The persistent water woes plaguing the CIDCO Colony in Sanpada Sector-10, along with several private societies and row house areas, are soon to become a thing of the past. The long-standing issue of inadequate water supply owing to low water pressure has prompted decisive action from the concerned authorities, leading to the initiation of a ground breaking project aimed at addressing the water scarcity in the area.
